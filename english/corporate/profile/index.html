Objectives The objectives of the company shall be to engage in the following business activities and to manage the business activities of companies (including foreign companies) or any other corporations, etc. that engage in the following business activities by owning shares or equity interests in those companies or corporations:
  1. (1)Contracting for civil engineering and building construction work
  2. (2)Research, survey, planning, designing and supervision related to the above
  3. (3)Research, survey, planning, designing, supervision and management for regional development, urban development, ocean development and environmental considerations, etc.
  4. (4)Housing business, and real estate dealings and leasing
  5. (5)Planning, designing, development, possession, maintenance, operation and management of a variety of public facilities and infrastructure such as roads, ports, water supply and sewage works, government office buildings, educational and cultural facilities, solid waste disposal facilities, medical facilities, sports facilities, hotels and restaurants
  6. (6)Environmental improvement business such as soil improvement, river and lake bottom sediment purification, lake and seawater improvement, and collecting, transporting, treatment and recycling of general waste and industrial waste
  7. (7)Supply electricity and energies, such as thermal
  8. (8)Fabrication, procurement, selling and leasing of construction machinery, equipment, temporary materials for construction and other manufactured items
  9. (9)Maintenance work and security service work for buildings and incidental facilities
  10. (10)Acquiring, licensing, and selling of industrial property rights, copyrights and other intellectual proprietary rights
  11. (11)Information processing service and development of computer software and hardware
  12. (12)Non-life insurance agency business, car insurance agency business under the Act on Securing Compensation for Automobile Accidents and life insurance selling business
  13. (13)Planning, designing, manufacturing, and sales related to plant factories
  14. (14)Producing, processing and sales of agricultural, forestry and fishery products, and research,study and development related to the production of agricultural, forestry and fishery products
  15. (15)Mediation and intermediation of corporate acquisitions, mergers, company splits, share exchanges and transfers, business transfers, capital alliances, business alliances, etc.
  16. (16)Mediation related to corporate management strategy formulation, organizational and business restructuring, corporate rehabilitation, etc.
  17. (17)Business related to worker dispatching and paid work referrals
  18. (18)Consulting business related to the above
  19. (19)Any and all other activities ancillary to the above
