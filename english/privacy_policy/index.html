Privacy Policy

The Tobishima Group recognizes that we have a social responsibility to protect personal information and ensure its trust, and we comply with laws and other norms related to the protection of personal information. We will appropriately handle the personal information of business associates, etc. as follows.

1: Collection and use of personal information
When our group collects personal information, we will clarify the purpose of use and use lawful and fair methods to the extent necessary to achieve that purpose.
In addition, personal information will only be used within the scope of the purpose of use and to the extent necessary for business performance.

2: Management and protection of personal information
Our group strives to protect personal information by ensuring the accuracy and up-to-dateness of personal information, and by taking measures to prevent leakage, falsification, loss, etc. of personal information due to unauthorized access.
Furthermore, unless required by law, personal information will not be provided to third parties without the prior consent of the individual.

3: Continuous improvement of management systems
Our group has established the "Personal Information Handling Regulations" and thoroughly disseminates them to our employees and other related parties, as well as continually maintaining and improving our personal information management system and protection measures.

4: Disclosure of retained personal data
Our group will respond in good faith to requests from customers and business associates to disclose, correct, suspend use, erase, or suspend providing information to third parties, etc. about themselves.

5: Use of Google Analytics
Our group has introduced Google's access analysis tool "Google Analytics" for the purpose of improving the quality of the websites we operate. Google Analytics uses cookies and mobile device identification information (advertising identifiers, etc.) to collect data, but this data is collected anonymously and does not identify individuals.
For more information about how Google Analytics collects and processes data about our website visitors, please see Google's ”Privacy & Terms”, especially the section "How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services."
